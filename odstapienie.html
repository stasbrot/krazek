Right of withdrawal

You have 14 days from receiving the goods to withdraw from the contract without giving any reason.

Please note: the right of withdrawal does not apply to custom-printed CDs, as they are made to your individual specifications. It applies only to the signature editions with ready-made prints.

How to withdraw

  1. Fill in the form below (you can copy it) or write your own statement containing the same information.
  2. Send an e-mail to shopkrazek.eu@gmail.com.
  3. Return the goods to the Seller's address within 14 days of sending the statement. Return shipping is at the Buyer's expense.
  4. Refund will be issued within 14 days of receiving the statement, using the same payment method.
